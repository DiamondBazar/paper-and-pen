Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the "Paper and Pen" application ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when it publishes videos to the owner's own social media accounts, including TikTok, through official APIs.

1. Who this App is for

The App is a personal content-automation tool operated solely by the owner of the Paper and Pen channel. It is not offered to third-party end users and does not collect data from the public.

2. Information we access

When the owner authorizes the App with TikTok, the App receives an access token that allows it to upload and post videos to the owner's own authorized account. The App may access basic account information (such as the account's open ID and profile basics) strictly to complete the publishing process.

3. Information we do NOT collect

The App does not collect, sell, or share personal data from viewers or any other users. It does not track browsing activity, and it does not build advertising profiles.

4. How information is used

Access tokens and account identifiers are used only to publish the owner's own video content. Tokens are stored locally on the owner's own computer and are never shared with third parties.

5. Data storage and security

Authorization tokens are stored locally in the owner's environment and are protected by the security of that device. We do not operate external servers that store user data.

6. Third-party services

The App interacts with TikTok and YouTube through their official APIs. Your use of those platforms is governed by their respective privacy policies.

7. Data retention and deletion

The owner may revoke the App's access at any time from their TikTok account settings, which invalidates the stored token. Locally stored tokens can be deleted at any time.

8. Children's privacy

The App is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
